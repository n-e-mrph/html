This table compares a few different popular frameworks based on their type and ease of use. Frameworks are a tool that sometimes require a learning curve. Before starting it is key to know the intended use for a framework in order to choose the right one for the project.

Although frameworks are designed to make web development easier, some are more intuitive than others. It all depends on the goals for what they want the website to do. Comparing the different frameworks in a table can help a developer choose the right one for their needs.

Some frameworks are designed and suited well for beginners, while other are designed for more complex applications. One framework might be very customizable and another might not allow as much customization, but instead its use is intended for building on top of the foundation that’s already been created, like with Foundation for instance.
