Nightruder: A Night Inteeriddpted

From the unhinged mind of Nebraska's video game bad boy, Ron Ronaldson

Legendary Adventure

Nightruder: A Night Inteeriddpted is an upcoming RPG which features both single and multiplayer options. Players can delve into an ever expanding open world as big as the Yellowstone National Park. Yes, the real Yellowstone.

Character Classes

You choose from ten unique character classes, each with different weapons, playstyles, and abilities. There's a class you'll love, whether your style is magic, brute strength, stealth, or wreaking havoc. Let your inner desires take you away on an intricately detailed journey.
